1. Term Life Shield Plan
Category: Insurance
Description: A pure term insurance providing high coverage at low premiums with no maturity benefits.
Ideal For: Individuals who want maximum financial protection for dependents.
Premium Range: ₹400–₹1,200 per month
Key Benefits: High sum assured, tax benefits, low cost.
Eligibility: Age 18–60, medical check may be required.

2. Income Protector Term Plan
Category: Insurance
Description: Offers monthly income to dependents for fixed years after policyholder’s death.
Ideal For: Families relying on a single breadwinner.
Premium Range: ₹500–₹1,500 per month
Key Benefits: Monthly income payouts, flexible tenure.
Eligibility: Age 21–55.

3. Whole Life Wealth Cover
Category: Insurance
Description: Life cover valid till age 99 with cash value accumulation.
Ideal For: Long-term wealth building + family protection.
Premium Range: ₹1,500–₹3,400 per month
Key Benefits: Lifetime cover, maturity lump sum, loan facility.
Eligibility: Age 18–60.

4. Guaranteed Endowment Savings Plan
Category: Insurance
Description: Combines life cover with guaranteed maturity payout for savings goals.
Ideal For: Users planning future expenses like marriage or education.
Premium Range: ₹800–₹2,500 per month
Key Benefits: Guaranteed maturity amount, bonuses.
Eligibility: Age 18–55.

5. Child Future Education Plan
Category: Insurance
Description: Provides planned payouts at education milestones. Premiums stop if parent dies.
Ideal For: Parents saving for child’s future.
Premium Range: ₹700–₹2,000 per month
Key Benefits: Milestone payouts, premium waiver.
Eligibility: Parent age 21–50.

6. Retirement Pension Builder Plan
Category: Insurance
Description: Builds a retirement corpus and converts it to monthly pension.
Ideal For: Users wanting post-retirement financial stability.
Premium Range: ₹1,000–₹3,000 per month
Key Benefits: Lifetime pension options, tax benefits.
Eligibility: Age 25–65.

7. ULIP Wealth Maximizer
Category: Insurance
Description: Investment + insurance policy with choice of equity or debt funds.
Ideal For: Users wanting high-growth market-linked investments.
Premium Range: ₹1,500–₹5,000 per month
Key Benefits: Market returns, partial withdrawals, fund switching.
Eligibility: Age 18–55.

8. Guaranteed Return Insurance Plan
Category: Insurance
Description: Offers risk-free fixed returns at maturity.
Ideal For: Low-risk investors.
Premium Range: ₹1,000–₹4,000 per month
Key Benefits: Guaranteed payout, tax benefits.
Eligibility: Age 18–60.

9. Critical Illness Rider
Category: Insurance
Description: Pays lump sum on diagnosis of listed serious illnesses like cancer, stroke, etc.
Ideal For: Users needing medical risk protection.
Premium Range: ₹300–₹700 per month
Key Benefits: One-time payout, 25–35 diseases covered.
Eligibility: Age 18–55.

10. Accidental Death Benefit Insurance
Category: Insurance
Description: Additional payout if death occurs due to accident.
Ideal For: Frequent travelers or risk-factor jobs.
Premium Range: ₹150–₹450 per month
Key Benefits: Extra cover, easy claim process.
Eligibility: Age 18–60.

11. Personal Accident Disability Cover
Category: Insurance
Description: Compensation for partial or permanent disability due to accidents.
Ideal For: Workers, bikers, high-mobility users.
Premium Range: ₹200–₹600 per month
Key Benefits: Disability income, medical reimbursements.
Eligibility: Age 18–60.

12. Home Loan Protection Plan
Category: Insurance
Description: Pays off outstanding home loan if the borrower dies.
Ideal For: Homeowners with EMIs.
Premium Range: ₹900–₹2,500 per month
Key Benefits: EMI protection, financial security for family.
Eligibility: Age 21–65.

13. Group Term Employee Plan
Category: Insurance
Description: Employer-provided collective life cover.
Ideal For: Organizations or startups.
Premium Range: ₹100–₹300 per employee per month
Key Benefits: Low cost, bulk coverage.
Eligibility: Companies with 5+ employees.

14. Family Protection Combo Plan
Category: Insurance
Description: Combines term insurance + critical illness + accidental cover in one policy.
Ideal For: Families wanting all-round protection.
Premium Range: ₹1,200–₹2,500 per month
Key Benefits: Multiple risk covers under one plan.
Eligibility: Age 18–55.

15. Refundable Term Plan (Return of Premium)
Category: Insurance
Description: Returns total premiums paid if policyholder survives the term.
Ideal For: Users who don’t want premiums to be "lost".
Premium Range: ₹1,000–₹2,800 per month
Key Benefits: Survival benefit refund.
Eligibility: Age 18–55.
