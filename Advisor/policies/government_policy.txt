1. Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY)
Category: Government
Description: Low-cost life insurance with ₹2 lakh cover.
Ideal For: Low-income individuals needing affordable cover.
Annual Premium: ₹330
Eligibility: Age 18–50.

2. Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)
Category: Government
Description: Accidental insurance providing upto ₹2 lakh cover.
Annual Premium: ₹20
Eligibility: Age 18–70.

3. Atal Pension Yojana (APY)
Category: Government
Description: Pension scheme offering guaranteed monthly pension after retirement.
Ideal For: Unorganized sector workers.
Premium Range: ₹100–₹500 per month
Eligibility: 18–40.

4. Ayushman Bharat – PMJAY
Category: Government
Description: Health insurance of ₹5 lakh per family per year for hospitalization.
Ideal For: Low-income families.
Premium: Free
Eligibility: Based on SECC economic criteria.

5. Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)
Category: Government
Description: Savings scheme for girl child with high interest rates.
Ideal For: Parents of girl children.
Premium: ₹250–₹12,500 monthly
Eligibility: Girl child <10 years.

6. PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi
Category: Government
Description: Income support of ₹6,000 per year to farmers.
Eligibility: Land-owning farmers.

7. National Pension System (NPS)
Category: Government
Description: Retirement savings scheme with market-linked growth.
Premium: Flexible (₹500–₹10,000 monthly).
Eligibility: Citizens aged 18–70.

8. Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana (RSBY)
Category: Government
Description: Health insurance for BPL families.
Coverage: ₹30,000 per year
Eligibility: BPL households.

9. PM Awas Yojana (PMAY)
Category: Government
Description: Subsidy on home loans for economically weaker sections.
Eligibility: EWS, LIG, MIG families.

10. Stand-Up India Scheme
Category: Government
Description: Loans for women and SC/ST entrepreneurs.
Eligibility: Age 18+, entrepreneur.

11. Mudra Loan (PMMY)
Category: Government
Description: Loans for small businesses (Shishu, Kishor, Tarun categories).
Range: ₹50,000–₹10 lakh.

12. Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)
Category: Government
Description: Crop insurance for farmers against natural calamities.
Eligibility: Farmers with crop loans.

13. Ujjwala Yojana
Category: Government
Description: LPG connection subsidy for rural households.
Eligibility: BPL families.

14. Senior Citizens Savings Scheme (SCSS)
Category: Government
Description: High interest rate savings for senior citizens.
Eligibility: Age 60+.

15. Digital Health Mission ID
Category: Government
Description: Unique health ID for digital medical records.
Eligibility: All citizens.
